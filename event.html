Maze-x

The team have to build an autonomous bot that can traverse a maze, while keeping track of directions. The bot has to analyze the maze in the dry run and traverse the maze in the shortest path in the actual run.

Rocket League

The event is basically RC Football. Each team has to build a car of the dimensions provided and has to compete against other teams bot in a soccer match . The team with maximum goals will win the match.
